VITA_MY_STORY_NARRATIVE_LAYER

CORE_IDEA
Preserving the human voice before it fades.
A digital sanctuary for the stories that define a family's legacy.

PURPOSE
To lower the barrier to storytelling.
We replace the blank page with simple, guided human questions, making it effortless to capture memories that would otherwise be lost.

SENTIMENT
Warm. Patient. Timeless.
The app feels like a quiet Sunday afternoon with a loved one. It is not a tool; it is a companion.

AUDIENCE
The Storytellers: Elders who want to leave something behind.
The Curators: Adult children capturing the legacy of their parents.
The Future: Grandchildren who will one day need to know where they came from.

REACH_&_IMPACT
Today: A simple moment of connection.
Tomorrow: A treasured digital heirloom.
Always: The preservation of identity, culture, and love.

DESIGN_TONE
Visual: Airy, editorial, high-trust. Serif typography (Libre Baskerville) meets clean modern spacing.
UX: "Breathing Room." No clutter. No urgency. Every interaction feels intentional and prestigious.
