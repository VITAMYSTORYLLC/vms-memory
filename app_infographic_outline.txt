VITA_MY_STORY_INFOGRAPHIC_OUTLINE

WHAT_IT_IS
A digital sanctuary for family legacy.
Simple, guided storytelling.
A private archive, not a social feed.

HOW_IT_WORKS
1. Choose a loved one to honor.
2. Receive one meaningful question at a time.
3. Speak, type, or upload a photo.
4. Build a timeless collection of memories.

WHY_IT_MATTERS
Because voices fade, but stories remain.
To bridge the gap between generations.
To ensure no chapter is ever lost.
